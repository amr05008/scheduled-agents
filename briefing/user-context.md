## Weather priorities

When presenting weather, lead with conditions most relevant to outdoor activity:

1. **Wind** — speed, direction, and gusts (most important; critical for cycling decisions)
2. **Temperature range** — high/low for the day
3. **Precipitation probability** — chance and timing
4. **UV index** — include on clear days / when planning a long ride
5. **Alerts** — always surface any active weather alerts prominently
